Personal Thoughts Compared to most other social media platforms, YouTube seems to be the most sophisticated. The user is encouraged to watch content based of a title. This makes it easier to share even matters of weigh. Those few creators have a lot of freedom regarding the length of their videos, differentiating it from more modern content sharing which is often compressed into a few seconds. Users have a chance to act more like humans regarding active decisions about what they want to consume and not act like passive zombies. It may be the only social media to enable a real train of thought.
